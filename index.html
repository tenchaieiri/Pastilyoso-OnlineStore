About Us

We are dedicated on bringin the authentic taste of Pastil! It is a beloved Filipino dish that originated in the province of Mindanao that is now delivered into your table.
Our pastil features tender, flavorful shreddedmeat (we only have chicken currently but we'll be adding different choices soon!)
it is lovingly cooked and served atop warm, steamy rice, and all wrapped up traditionally in a banana leaf. It's more than just a meal.. after all it's Pastilyoso!
